[ secrets ]  

  • lore:
    • unlocking lore will give you dev mode for while in the game
    • you need to get 7 undefined's from the tech powerups (while not in experiment mode)
    • undefined's are randomly found in tech powerups and have a rgb orb icon and text
    • after getting all 7 undefined's you need to beat the game
    • after beating the game a tunnel will open located at the end of the final level
    • after entering the tunnel, you will be placed in the lore level and given dev mode
